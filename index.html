SNAXKREW is an open gaming community founded in October 2014, now going almost 3 years strong. Most of our veterans all met each other playing a small MOBA called Strife, and at our peak we held 200+ members in the game from regions all over the world, and were the largest thriving community that was always welcoming to new recruits. Since then, we have moved on to playing many different games.
