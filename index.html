SNAXKREW is a thriving open gaming community founded in October 2014. Going on almost 3 years now, we have had the pleasure of recruiting members far and wide always welcoming new ones along the way! Our main event is a weekly game night in which we group up and chose from a fun selection of voted-for games, sharing laughs and great times! Currently, we are expanding our roster in high hopes of experiencing many more new and exciting chapters!
